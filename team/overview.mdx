## What is Team Workflow?

Team Workflow is Revol's upcoming CRM and team productivity suite. It turns AI-generated leads into a structured sales pipeline with tasks, goals, and performance analytics.

While the **Agents Platform** handles customer conversations, Team Workflow handles **what happens after** — lead qualification, task assignment, goal tracking, and team performance reporting.

## Lead Flow: From Agent to Team

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI Agent Handles Conversation">
    Your AI agent chats with a visitor on the website, Telegram, or phone. It answers questions, qualifies the lead, and collects contact information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lead Created Automatically">
    When the conversation meets your criteria (e.g., visitor asked for pricing, left a phone number), a lead is created in the pipeline automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Team Takes Over">
    A team member is assigned to the lead. They see the full conversation history, visitor tracking data, and agent's assessment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track to Completion">
    Move leads through pipeline stages, add tasks, set follow-up reminders, and track conversion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How It Connects to Agents Platform

Team Workflow extends the Agents Platform with human-in-the-loop capabilities:

- **Conversation history** — full chat transcripts and call recordings attached to each lead
- **Visitor tracking** — session data, pages visited, UTM source linked to leads
- **Agent insights** — AI-generated lead summaries and qualification scores
- **Webhook integration** — push leads to external CRMs via the existing webhook system
