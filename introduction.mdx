## Key Features

- **AI Agents** — Build intelligent assistants with custom workflows, knowledge bases, and voice capabilities
- **Multi-Channel** — Connect via website widget, VoIP, Telegram, Viber, and more
- **Tracking & Analytics** — Monitor visitor sessions, conversions, and campaign performance
- **Marketplace** — Share and install workflow templates
- **Billing** — Flexible plans with token-based usage tracking

Revol consists of three main components:
