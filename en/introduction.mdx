## What is Revol?

Revol is a platform for building a managed sales department. Your managers and AI work in a single system — following the same sales scripts, using the same knowledge base, and communicating with customers through the same channels.

Revol is **not a chatbot**. It's a sales process management system with AI as a force multiplier. Three operating modes let you choose the right balance:

1. **Passive** — your managers handle conversations, AI provides real-time hints: what to say next, which product to suggest, where the script is being deviated from.

2. **Active** — AI leads the conversation autonomously: follows the sales script, qualifies leads, answers product questions, books meetings. Your team reviews results, not chats.

3. **Hybrid** — managers work during business hours, AI picks up nights and weekends. Or AI handles advertising traffic while managers focus on VIP clients. You decide what goes where.

All three modes share the same knowledge base, the same sales scripts, and the same analytics — so switching between them is seamless.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Company">
    Register and set up your company — add your business name, website, phone number, and logo. The company is the foundation: all products, files, agents, and integrations belong to it.


      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/company-setup-light.png" alt="Company creation" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/company-setup-dark.png" alt="Company creation" />

  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Your Knowledge Base">
    Fill it with everything your team and AI need to sell:

    - **Products & services** — name, description, photos, pricing, availability
    - **Documents** — PDFs, Word files, spreadsheets, price lists
    - **Links** — website pages and external resources (content fetched automatically)
    - **Text snippets** — FAQ, objection handling scripts, policies
    - **Media** — videos (transcribed to text) and photos (OCR extracted)

    All content is automatically chunked and converted into vector embeddings. Your managers find answers in seconds, AI cites your actual materials.


      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/file-manager-light.png" alt="File Manager" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/file-manager-dark.png" alt="File Manager" />

  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up a Sales Script">
    Use the visual workflow editor to build your conversation scenario: greeting → qualification → offer → close. Every step can include conditions, tool calls, and branching logic.

    Build the script once — both your managers and AI follow it automatically. New team members get a ready playbook from day one.


      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/agents-workflow-editor-light.png" alt="Workflow editor" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/agents-workflow-editor-dark.png" alt="Workflow editor" />

  </Step>
  <Step title="Create & Train an AI Agent">
    Create an agent, choose an LLM provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, or Groq), and select which knowledge sources it should learn from — specific products, documents, links, or text.

    Hit **Train** and Revol generates vector embeddings so your agent answers questions based on your actual data — not generic responses.


      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/agents-knowledge-base-light.png" alt="Agent knowledge base configuration" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/agents-knowledge-base-dark.png" alt="Agent knowledge base configuration" />

  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Communication Channels">
    Bring all customer touchpoints into one inbox:

    - **Website widget** — embed a single script for chat and voice
    - **Telephony** — Twilio, Ringostat, or Binotel for phone calls
    - **Messengers** — Telegram, WhatsApp Business, Instagram DM, Facebook Messenger
    - **CRM** — Custom Webhook to send lead data to any system
    - **Google Workspace** — Gmail, Google Docs, Sheets, Drive, Calendar, and Meet
    - **Ad Campaigns** — Google Ads and Facebook Ads analytics

    Your team and AI work in the same channels with shared conversation history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch & Monitor">
    Choose your operating mode — passive, active, or hybrid — and start. Monitor everything in real time: which manager closed how many deals, where leads are dropping off, which scripts convert best. AI picks up when your team can't — nights, weekends, traffic spikes.


      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/conversations-dashboard-light.png" alt="Conversations dashboard" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/conversations-dashboard-dark.png" alt="Conversations dashboard" />

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who Is Revol For?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sales Teams" icon="users">
    Standardize your sales process with scripts, give managers real-time AI hints, and track KPI per person. New hires get the knowledge base + AI assistance from day one — no weeks of training.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="E-commerce" icon="cart-shopping">
    AI knows your entire product catalog — descriptions, photos, availability, pricing. It handles product questions, recommends alternatives, and qualifies leads 24/7. Your team focuses on closing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Service Companies" icon="headset">
    Appointment booking, FAQ, and support requests handled around the clock. Voice-enabled agents answer phone calls. Complex cases get routed to human operators automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Agencies" icon="buildings">
    Manage up to 10 client companies from one account. Each gets isolated agents, knowledge bases, scripts, and analytics. Reuse proven workflow templates across clients via the Marketplace.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Key Capabilities

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Team + AI">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Sales Scripts** | Visual workflow editor — build conversation scenarios with drag-and-drop |
    | **3 Operating Modes** | Passive (AI hints), Active (AI leads), Hybrid (split by channel/hours) |
    | **Script Compliance** | AI tracks deviations from the script and flags missed opportunities |
    | **Onboarding** | New managers get knowledge base + AI hints from day one |
    | **Conversation Audit** | Every conversation recorded, analyzed, scored |

    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Multi-Provider LLM** | Choose per-agent or per-node: OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, or Groq |

    | **Voice** | Real-time STT/TTS with interrupt detection and streaming |
    | **Multi-Channel** | Website widget, VoIP, Telegram, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger |
    | **Marketplace** | Install ready-made workflow templates or publish your own |
