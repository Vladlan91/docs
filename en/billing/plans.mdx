## Overview

Revol uses a **token-based billing** model powered by **Stripe**. Every AI interaction consumes tokens — the more your agents work, the more tokens are used. Choose a plan that fits your volume, and purchase add-on packs if you need more.

## Model Access

Each plan determines which AI models are available:

| Tier | Models | Plans |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Standard** | GPT-4o Mini, Claude 3.5 Haiku, Gemini 2.0 Flash, Llama 3.1 8B | All plans |
| **Premium** | GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Gemini 2.5 Pro, Llama 3.3 70B | Premium and Professional |

Standard models are faster and consume standard tokens. Premium models offer higher quality responses and consume premium tokens. On the Free plan, premium models are locked with an upgrade prompt.

## Payment via Stripe

All payments are processed securely through **Stripe**. Revol does not store your card details — everything is handled by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure.

### Subscribing to a Plan

1. Go to **Billing** → **Plans** and click **Select Plan** on the plan you want
2. You are redirected to a **Stripe Checkout** page — enter your card details and confirm
3. After successful payment, your plan activates immediately and all new limits apply
4. A subscription is created with automatic monthly renewal

### Managing Your Subscription

Navigate to **Billing** → **Overview** and click **Manage Subscription**:

- **View billing period** — see your current cycle start and end dates
- **Auto-renewal toggle** — turn off to cancel at the end of the current period (you keep access until then)
- **Resume** — if you canceled but the period hasn't ended, you can re-enable auto-renewal
- **Payment methods** — click **Manage Payment Methods** to open the Stripe Customer Portal where you can update cards, view receipts, and manage billing details

<Warning>
  When you cancel a subscription, it remains active until the end of the current billing period (grace period). After that, you are automatically downgraded to the Free plan. All your data is preserved, but limits are reduced.
</Warning>

### Upgrading and Downgrading

- **Upgrade** — takes effect immediately. You are charged the new plan price, prorated for the remaining days in the current period.
- **Downgrade** — takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You keep full access to the higher plan until the period ends.

## Add-on Packs

Need more resources without upgrading? Purchase one-time add-on packs:

| Pack | What You Get | Price |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **Token Pack S** | 5,000,000 standard tokens | $4 |
| **Token Pack M** | 20,000,000 standard tokens | $15 |
| **Token Pack L** | 50,000,000 standard tokens | $35 |
| **Premium Token Pack** | 2,000,000 premium tokens | $20 |
| **Voice Pack** | 60 STT minutes + 100,000 TTS characters | $3 |
| **Storage Pack** | 10 GB additional storage | $1 |

Packs are purchased via Stripe Checkout (one-time payment, not recurring) and activate immediately after payment.

<Note>
  Add-on packs expire **3 months** after purchase. Unused resources do not roll over. When you have multiple packs, the oldest-expiring pack is consumed first.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Packs supplement your plan limits — they are used only after your included plan quota is exhausted. For example, if your Premium plan includes 5M standard tokens and you purchased a Token Pack S (5M), you effectively have 10M tokens for that period.
</Tip>

All payment history is available in **Billing** → **Invoices**.

Each invoice shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Invoice #** | Auto-generated number (e.g., INV-202603-42) |
| **Date** | Payment date |
| **Description** | Plan name or pack type |
| **Period** | Billing period covered |
| **Amount** | Payment amount in USD |
| **Status** | Paid, Pending, or Failed |
| **PDF** | Download link to the Stripe-generated invoice PDF |

Filter invoices by date range, type (subscription or pack), and payment status.

Summary cards at the top show: **Total Paid** (all-time), **Transaction Count**, and **Active Plan**.

## What Happens at the Limit

| Resource | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| Standard / Premium Tokens | Agent stops responding until the next billing cycle or until you purchase a pack |
| Storage | Cannot upload new files to the File Manager |
| Voice (STT/TTS) | Voice features are disabled, text chat continues working |
| Conversations | New conversations are blocked, existing ones continue |
| Knowledge Docs | Cannot add new documents to agent knowledge bases |

Revol sends notifications at **80%** (warning) and **90%** (critical) usage via email and in-app notification, so you can act before hitting the limit.
