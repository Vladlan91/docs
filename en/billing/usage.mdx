## Usage Dashboard

Monitor your resource consumption in **Admin Panel** → **Billing**.

The dashboard shows:
- Token usage (standard & premium)
- Voice minutes and characters
- Storage consumption
- Platform limits (companies, agents, conversations)

## How Tokens Work

Every AI interaction consumes tokens:

- **Input tokens** — Your message + context (knowledge base chunks, system prompt)
- **Output tokens** — Agent's response

Different models have different costs per token. Standard models are more affordable, while premium models offer higher quality.

## Usage Events

Each billable action creates a **usage event** with:
- Timestamp
- Company and agent
- Event type (chat, voice, embedding)
- Resource type and amount
- Calculated cost

## Notifications

Revol sends notifications when you approach your limits:
- **80%** — Warning notification
- **90%** — Critical notification

## Overage

When you exceed your plan limits:
- Standard and premium tokens: agent stops responding
- Storage: cannot upload new files
- Voice: voice features disabled

Purchase add-on packs to restore access immediately.
