## What is Creative Platform?

Creative Platform is Revol's upcoming AI content generation suite. It brings photo, video, sound, and creative generation tools into one workspace — alongside automated publishing to social media.

While the **Agents Platform** (PRO v1.0) focuses on customer-facing AI assistants, Creative Platform focuses on **content production** — helping marketing teams generate and publish visual and audio content at scale.

## How It Connects to Agents Platform

Creative Platform and Agents Platform share the same company data:

- **Same knowledge base** — use your business data for content generation
- **Same billing** — unified token consumption across both platforms
- **Same dashboard** — switch between platforms with one click in the sidebar
