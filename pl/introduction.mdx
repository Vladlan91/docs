## What is Revol?

Revol is an AI-first platform for managing customer interactions through intelligent agents. It serves two fundamentally different use cases from a single dashboard:

1. **AI handles communication** — the agent autonomously responds to customers across all channels: website chat, phone calls, messengers. It follows your workflow, searches the knowledge base, calls tools, and resolves requests without human involvement.

2. **Your team handles communication, AI monitors** — your operators talk to customers as usual, while the agent works in the background as a quality auditor. It analyzes every conversation for compliance with communication standards, tracks whether scripts are followed, identifies missed opportunities, and surfaces insights — without ever responding to customers directly.

These two approaches can be combined — the AI handles routine interactions (FAQ, product questions, scheduling) while your team takes over for complex or sensitive cases, with the agent continuously analyzing everything.

Unlike generic chatbot builders, Revol combines a **visual workflow editor**, **RAG-powered knowledge bases**, **real-time voice capabilities**, and **visitor analytics** into one unified platform. Whether your agent is actively communicating or passively monitoring, it knows your products, follows configurable conversation flows, calls external tools, and tracks every interaction for optimization.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Company">
    Register and set up your company — add your business name, website, phone number, and logo. The company is the foundation: all products, files, agents, and integrations belong to it.

    
      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/company-setup-light.png" alt="Company creation" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/company-setup-dark.png" alt="Company creation" />
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Products & Knowledge Base">
    Fill your company with data that your AI agent will learn from:

    - **Products & services** — name, description, photos, pricing, availability
    - **Documents** — PDFs, Word files, spreadsheets
    - **Links** — website pages and external resources (content fetched automatically)
    - **Text snippets** — FAQ, policies, custom instructions
    - **Media** — videos (transcribed to text) and photos (OCR extracted)

    All uploaded content is automatically chunked and converted into vector embeddings for RAG retrieval.

    
      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/file-manager-light.png" alt="File Manager" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/file-manager-dark.png" alt="File Manager" />
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create & Train an AI Agent">
    Create an agent, choose a use case (customer support, sales, scheduling, lead qualification), and select an LLM provider — OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, or Groq.

    Then pick which **knowledge sources** your agent should learn from — specific products, documents, links, or text. Hit **Train** and Revol generates vector embeddings so your agent can answer questions based on your actual data.

    
      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/agents-knowledge-base-light.png" alt="Agent knowledge base configuration" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/agents-knowledge-base-dark.png" alt="Agent knowledge base configuration" />
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Workflow (Optional)">
    For complex conversation scenarios, use the visual workflow editor. Build multi-step flows with LLM nodes, conditions, tool calls, and voice interactions — all connected via drag-and-drop.

    
      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/agents-workflow-editor-light.png" alt="Workflow editor" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/agents-workflow-editor-dark.png" alt="Workflow editor" />
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Communication Channels">
    Integrate your agent with the channels your customers use:

    - **Website widget** — embed a single script for chat and voice
    - **Telephony** — Twilio, Ringostat, or Binotel for phone calls
    - **Messengers** — Telegram, WhatsApp Business, Instagram DM, Facebook Messenger
    - **CRM** — Custom Webhook to send lead data to any system
    - **Google Workspace** — Gmail, Google Docs, Sheets, Drive, Calendar, and Meet
    - **Ad Campaigns** — Google Ads and Facebook Ads analytics

    Each agent can be connected to multiple channels simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy & Monitor">
    Install the widget script on your website. In **Active** mode, your agent handles conversations autonomously. In **Passive** mode, your team communicates while the agent audits and analyzes every interaction. Monitor everything in real time — read transcripts, listen to call recordings, track visitor sessions, and optimize based on analytics.

    
      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/conversations-dashboard-light.png" alt="Widget script installation" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/conversations-dashboard-dark.png" alt="Widget script installation" />
    
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who Is Revol For?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="E-commerce" icon="cart-shopping">
    Automate product questions, order tracking, and recommendations. Your agent knows your entire product catalog — descriptions, photos, availability, pricing — and can guide customers through purchases using real-time data via tool calls.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Service Companies" icon="headset">
    Handle appointment booking, FAQ, and support requests 24/7. Upload your service descriptions, pricing lists, and policies. Voice-enabled agents can answer phone calls and route complex issues to human operators.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Agencies" icon="buildings">
    Manage multiple client accounts from one dashboard. Each company gets isolated agents, knowledge bases, and analytics. Use the Marketplace to reuse proven workflow templates across clients.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="SaaS Products" icon="cloud">
    Embed an AI support agent in your product. Train it on your documentation, API reference, and changelog. Reduce support tickets while improving response quality.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Key Capabilities

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Customer-Facing AI">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Multi-Provider LLM** | Choose per-agent or per-node: OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, or Groq |
    | **RAG Knowledge Base** | Vector search via pgvector — train on products, docs, links, text |
    | **Visual Workflow** | Drag-and-drop node editor with conditions, tool calls, and branching |
    | **Voice** | Real-time STT/TTS with interrupt detection and streaming |
    | **Tool Calling** | Agents can call external APIs, query databases, and perform actions |
    | **Multi-Channel** | Website widget, VoIP (Twilio, Ringostat, Binotel), Telegram, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Analytics & Tracking">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Session Tracking** | Visitor sessions with device, location, and UTM data |
    | **Event Tracking** | Custom events — clicks, form submissions, phone calls |
    | **Campaign Analytics** | UTM-based campaign grouping and conversion tracking |
    | **Triggers** | Automated overlays based on time, scroll, UTM, device type |
    | **Phone Swap** | Dynamic phone number replacement for call tracking |
    | **Conversation Linking** | Connect tracking sessions with chat conversations |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Platform">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Company Management** | Multi-company support with isolated data per company |
    | **Product Catalog** | Manage products and services that agents learn from |
    | **File Manager** | Upload documents, links, text, video, and photos for knowledge bases |
    | **Marketplace** | Browse, install, and publish workflow templates |
    | **Billing** | Token-based metering with separate quotas for standard and premium models |
    | **Widget Customization** | Colors, labels, placement, terms & conditions — configurable per agent |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
